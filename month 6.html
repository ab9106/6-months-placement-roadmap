Company-Specific Practice

  • Glassdoor - Company-Specific Interview Questions
    Link: https://www.glassdoor.com/Interview/index.htm
    Recommended for: Glassdoor provides a wealth of company-specific interview questions, including coding, technical, and HR questions from real candidates. Research target companies and understand their interview patterns.
  • LeetCode Discussion Forums - Company-Specific Questions
    Link: https://leetcode.com/discuss/
    Recommended for: Check the company-specific discussion forums on LeetCode for coding questions frequently asked in interviews for companies like Google, Amazon, Microsoft, etc.
  • InterviewBit - Company-Specific Interview Questions
    Link: https://www.interviewbit.com/
    Recommended for: Provides targeted preparation for interviews at specific companies. You can practice questions, and review detailed explanations to get an idea of company-specific questions.
