6-Month Placement Roadmap

Month 1: Foundation Building

Focus on the basics:

  • Learn core Data Structures and Algorithms.
  • Understand CS fundamentals: OS, DBMS, and OOP.
  • Strengthen problem-solving skills.

Month 2: Problem-Solving & CS Concepts

Focus on intermediate problem-solving:

  • Practice medium-level problems on LeetCode/Codeforces.
  • Learn Dynamic Programming, Graphs, and Greedy Algorithms.
  • Master computer networks and system design basics.

Month 3: Intermediate-Level Preparation

Build on what you've learned:

  • Tackle harder coding problems on HackerRank/Codeforces.
  • Dive into advanced topics like Trie, Segment Trees, and Disjoint Sets.
  • Begin exploring low-level system design concepts.

Month 4: Advanced Coding & Competitive Programming

Level up your coding game:

  • Regularly compete in coding contests (e.g., CodeChef, AtCoder).
  • Learn advanced algorithms and techniques (e.g., Backtracking, Bit Manipulation).
  • Practice mock interviews on platforms like InterviewBit or Pramp.

Month 5: Full-Fledged Placement Prep

Focus on placement-specific preparation:

  • Revisit frequently asked interview problems.
  • Brush up on key concepts of OOP, OS, DBMS, and Networking.
  • Prepare answers for HR and behavioral interview questions.

Month 6: Final Touches & Company-Specific Prep

Get ready for your dream job:

  • Focus on company-specific coding problems.
  • Revise notes on core CS concepts and systems design.
  • Mock interviews with friends or mentors to gain confidence.
